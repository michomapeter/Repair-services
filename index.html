OUR SERVICES

Change your oil regularly

Every engine needs regularly the oil is responsible for keeping the different components of your engine moving cohesively with minimal friction .Overtime oil gets dirty and needs to be changed to keep your engine healthy

Check your battery charge

While many batteries have warranties its not uncommon for batteries to only last around two or three years without needing replacement.When you change your oil also dont forget to check your batteries car voltage

Replace your air filter

the filter is responsible for removing debris from the air that is pumped into your engine as you rack up the miles on your car ,your airfilter will become inundated with these debris and need replacement. We strongly advice changing your air filter 15000 to 20000miles but you may have to do sooner if you drive through dusty areas

Replace old spark plugs

When one or more spark plugs arent functioning,your engine has to work harder causing inconsistent performance, have the spark plugs regularly inspected and replace any that are dying or completely burn out

Check hoses and belts

air hoses and timing and serpentine belts generlly last upto 60000miles or more but can and will usually become a concern after that

Replace Worn-Down Brake Pads

Most vehicles use disc brakes-ceramic pads that press against a metal disc inorder to stop your car.The ceramic pads will wear down over time so that they will need replacement
